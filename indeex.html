THE HIGHER SCHOOL OF TECHNOLOGY ESSAOUIRA


The Higher School of Technology in Essaouira is an educational institution located in Essaouira, Morocco. It offers a variety of technical and vocational programs aimed at preparing students for careers in fields such as engineering, technology, and applied sciences. The school is known for its focus on practical skills development, hands-on learning experiences, and industry-relevant curriculum. It often collaborates with local businesses and industries to provide students with internship opportunities and real-world projects. The campus is equipped with modern facilities and resources to support students' academic pursuits and extracurricular activities. The Higher School of Technology in Essaouira is committed to providing quality education and preparing graduates to meet the demands of the workforce.
